About Us

Established in 1995, Sunrise High School is dedicated to fostering a love for lifelong learning. Our passionate teachers provide a safe and engaging environment where every child can shine. We believe in strong academics supported by sports, arts, and community involvement.

Our commitment: Respect, curiosity, growth, and integrity.
